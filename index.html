Built on Legacy.
Driven by Industrial Excellence.

SAGUN is a leading manufacturer of Industrial and Edible Grade Acid Casein in India, known for consistent quality and reliable supply.

With roots in dairy since 1935 and a focused expansion into casein manufacturing in 1999, we have played a key role in transforming Morena into the “Casein Capital of India.” Today, SAGUN serves multiple industries across domestic and international markets.

Our facility combines traditional expertise with modern chemical processing to deliver products that meet the rigorous standards of global manufacturers, ensuring precision, consistency, and reliability in every batch.
